# Microsoft

![Microsoft](pathname:///img/partner-microsoft.png)

Microsoft regularly opens its doors to students to learn coding and tour their facilities. They asked Team BrainPad to help create and conduct a coding session to stimulate the students creativity and open doors to possibilities.

---

# Intel

![Intel](pathname:///img/partner-intel.png)

BrainPad was the focus of the Intel Summer STEM Camp. Students were engaged during the sessions and with each creating their own projects by the end of the camp.

---
# State of Michigan

![Michigan](pathname:///img/partner-michigan.png)

Team BrainPad was invited to the State of Michigan Capitol building to interview students about their technology projects and show-off projects made by students using BrainPad.

---

# Notre Dame Preparatory School and Marist Academy

![Notre Dame](pathname:///img/partner-notredame.png)

The kids from the Notre Dame School use the BrainPad system in their STEM curriculum, so much so their instructor has conducted several coding classes and events to spread the word to other educators.


---

# Beverly Hills Academy

![BHA](pathname:///img/partner-bha.png)

Beverly Hills Academy recognized the value of coding things and added BrainPad to its “After School Coding Nights”.


---

# SquareOne Education Network

![Squareone](pathname:///img/partner-squareone.png)

SquareOne’s mission is to “Develop the next generation technical workforce by empowering teachers with real-world resources and engaging students through authentic hands-on, project-based learning“. BrainPad is one of their tools to achieve this goal.


---

# Girls Who Code

![Girls Who Code](pathname:///img/partner-girlswhocode.png)

Girls Who Code is a non-profit organization that values diversity, equity, and inclusion as essential to their mission. Using the BrainPad was a natural choice to get everyone excited and interested.


---

# CODE313

![CODE313](pathname:///img/partner-code313.png)

CODE313 is a STEM.org Accredited non-profit organization with a mission to provide equitable access to STEAM education. BrainPad is used as one of their training components.


---

# Detroit Pistons

![Pistons](pathname:///img/partner-pistons.png)

The Detroit Pistons STEAM slam is a regular hosted event for a high energy day of hands-on science, technology, engineering, art, and math activities.


---

# Everbest Media – Malaysia

![Everbest](pathname:///img/partner-everbest.png)

EBM strongly values human development as it is critical to a country’s survival and believes that technology is one of the best media for increasing knowledge and enhancing the skills of individuals and society. BrainPad’s core educational value align perfectly with their objectives.


---

# Ballarpur Institute Of Technology – India 

![Ballarpur](pathname:///img/partner-bit.png)

Ballarpur Institute Of Technology(BIT) is a technical institution which is approved by AICTE (All India Council of Technical Education), New Delhi and has got the approval from Govt. of Maharashtra. BrainPad is designed to inspire and create future innovators.
